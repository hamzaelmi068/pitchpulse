# Data Sources Evaluation Prompt

Use this prompt when evaluating or integrating sports, news, or AI providers.

## Goal

Assess whether a provider is suitable for PitchPulse's World Cup 2026 use cases and identify the integration shape needed to consume it safely.

## Evaluation criteria

- Coverage for World Cup fixtures, events, standings, venues, and player stats
- Freshness and reliability of live updates
- Historical data availability if needed for comparisons or AI context
- Rate limits, pricing, authentication model, and operational constraints
- Ease of normalizing the provider into stable internal models

## Expected output

- Recommendation with reasoning
- Known gaps or risks
- Suggested normalized entities or adapter boundaries
- Any licensing or quota concerns that affect production viability
